<h3>Data</h3>
<p><strong>IRS Form 990 Data Cleaning For Non-Profit Hospitals</strong> - This dataset provides cleaned IRS Form 990 data for non-profit hospitals (2011-2022) with three integrated components: organizational and financial information, facility-level details (financial assistance and community benefit policies), and joint venture/management company information. The datasets can be merged on organization identifier and year for comprehensive analysis of hospital financial performance, operations, and organizational structures. Available <a href="https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/y3s6mxt7cm/2" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
